More about Us

As one of the country's 21 SETAs, EWSETA is your go-to source for top-notch education and training -

Established under the Skills Development Act and reporting directly to the Honourable Minister of Higher Education, Science and Technology, we're dedicated to helping you achieve your career goals and make your mark on this dynamic industry.

Our portal is your one-stop-shop for all the information and resources you need to explore the countless opportunities available in the Energy & Water sector. From cutting-edge innovation to sustainable practices and everything in between, we've got you covered with expert insights and guidance.

So why wait? Let's dive in and discover your potential today!

Psychometric Tests

Are you a high school student struggling to decide which career path to take? With so many options available, it can be overwhelming and confusing.
That’s where psychometric testing comes in. By taking a psychometric test, you can identify your strengths, weaknesses, interests, and personality traits, which can help you determine which careers are best suited for you.
These tests provide valuable insights into your skills and abilities, giving you the knowledge and confidence you need to make informed decisions about your future.

Why We Built This Site

We are committed to working with stakeholders across the country, including government, businesses, and communities, to find solutions to the challenges facing our water and energy systems. We believe by working together, we can ensure a brighter and more sustainable future for South Africa.

We are proud of the progress we have made in providing access to clean water and energy for all South Africans, and we will continue to work towards a future where every person has access to these essential resources.
